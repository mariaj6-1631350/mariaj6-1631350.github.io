As the capital of The Republic of Ireland, Dublin is one of the most popular places to visit in Ireland. It has many historical landmarks and unique pubs to check out!

Known for it's music, Galway sits on the west coast of Ireland. There are many fun shops to look at and interesting streets to walk down.

Belfast

Belfast is important to Ireland's history. It is the capital of Northern Ireland and it sits on the River Lagan. It is also where the Titanic was built!

Killarney has some of the most classic Irish scenery, especially if you travel the Ring of Kerry or visit Killarney National Park.

Cork City

Cork City holds one of the most popular universities in Ireland. If you travel just outside the city, you can visit Blarney Castle as well. If you kiss the Blarney Stone, you just might get the gift of the gab.

Cobh

Cobh is a beautiful city on the coast of Ireland. It is known for being the last port that the Titanic visited. It has many colorful streets to walk through and coastal scenery to enjoy.
